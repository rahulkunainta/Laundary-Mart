About LaundryMart

India’s most trusted and premium laundry & dry-cleaning service, redefining fabric care with state-of-the-art technology, world-class machines, and expert garment specialists. At LaundryMart, every fabric is treated with precision—using eco-friendly, dermatologically safe detergents, advanced stain-removal science, and hygienic wash processes designed to extend fabric life and preserve original texture, color, and softness.

From luxury designer wear and delicate couture garments to everyday essentials, linens, footwear, and household fabrics, our professional team ensures meticulous care and flawless results. With real-time order tracking, seamless doorstep pickup & delivery, and strict 7-step quality checks, we deliver a premium, worry-free laundry experience tailored for modern lifestyles.

Experience freshness, hygiene, convenience, and unmatched fabric care—crafted to make your life easier and your wardrobe truly timeless.

Our Journey

LaundryMart began with a simple idea — to make laundry easier, faster, and more reliable for every household. What started as a small facility with a commitment to quality has now grown into one of India’s most trusted names in premium fabric care. From day one, our mission has been clear: to elevate everyday laundry into a modern, seamless, and truly delightful experience.

As advancements in fabric care technology emerged, we embraced them wholeheartedly. With world-class machines, eco-friendly detergents, certified garment specialists, and strict quality protocols, we introduced a level of precision and care that transformed traditional laundry into a luxury service. Every garment — from delicate couture wear to daily essentials — receives meticulous attention founded on science, hygiene, and craftsmanship.

Over the years, we expanded our services to more than 30+ cities, growing through the trust and love of our customers. Each milestone strengthened our dedication to reliability, transparency, and convenience. Features like real-time order tracking, doorstep pickup & delivery, and our signature 7-step care process set new standards in the industry.

Today, LaundryMart stands at the forefront of fabric-care innovation — not just as a laundry service, but as a partner in helping families and professionals save time, maintain hygiene, and preserve their wardrobe for years. Our journey continues with one promise: to deliver unmatched quality and convenience, while constantly evolving to meet the needs of modern living.
