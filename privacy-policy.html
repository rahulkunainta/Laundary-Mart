Privacy Policy

Your privacy & trust matter to us.

1. Introduction

LaundryMart (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data when you use our website, mobile app, or services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal details (name, phone number, address)
  • Order information and service history
  • Payment details (secured through encrypted gateways)
  • Location details for pickup & delivery
  • Technical data (IP address, device details, browser info)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your laundry orders
  • To provide customer support
  • To improve website/app performance
  • To send updates, alerts, and promotional offers
  • To ensure service safety and fraud prevention

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or trade your personal data. Your information may be shared only with:

  • Trusted delivery partners
  • Payment gateway providers
  • Legal authorities (only when required)

5. Data Security

Your information is protected using SSL encryption, secure servers, and strict access control measures. We continuously monitor and upgrade our security systems.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of promotional messaging

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The revised date will always be mentioned at the top of this page.
